Every mile is money.

Routio logs your business drives automatically and turns them into IRS-ready mileage deductions — no account, private to you, synced across your devices through your own iCloud.

What it does

  • Automatic drive detection — trips are tracked in the background; no buttons to press.
  • One-tap classification — swipe each drive business or personal; rules learn your routes.
  • IRS-ready reports — Schedule C summaries and PDF/CSV exports at tax time.
  • Private by design — your drive data stays on your device and your private iCloud. It's never sold and never sent to our servers.
