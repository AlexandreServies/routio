Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

How does automatic tracking work?

Grant "Always" location once and Routio detects when you start and stop driving, logging each trip in the background. You don't open the app or press anything — just review and classify drives whenever you like.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no password, no email required. Your data lives on your device and your own private iCloud, so it follows you across your iPhone and iPad automatically.

Is my location data sold or shared?

Never. Your drives are stored on your device and your private iCloud (Apple's CloudKit). They're never sent to our servers and never sold to anyone. Privacy is the whole point.

How is this different from MileIQ?

Routio includes a full IRS Schedule C tax package (MileIQ doesn't), needs no account, never sells your data, and costs $59.99/yr — exactly what MileIQ used to charge before its ~50% price hike to $89.99.

What does it cost?

$59.99/year (about $5/month) or $7.99/month, with a 7-day free trial on the annual plan. Cancel anytime in Settings.

Switching from another app?

Bring your history with you. Export your trips as a CSV from MileIQ, Everlance, Stride, or QuickBooks, then use Import history in Routio to add your past drives. You keep your whole year instead of starting over.

Will it drain my battery?

Routio uses iOS's efficient motion + location services designed for all-day background use, so it's built to be light on battery while still catching every drive.
