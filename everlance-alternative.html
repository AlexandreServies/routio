Questions

Everlance vs Routio.

Do I have to link my bank like Everlance?

No. Routio is mileage-first and never asks to connect your bank. It tracks your drives and builds your deduction — that's it. If you want full expense and bank tracking, a broader tool fits; if you want clean, private mileage + taxes, Routio is simpler.

Is Routio's tracking automatic too?

Yes — automatic, background drive detection with smart auto-classification that learns your regular routes, so most trips sort themselves with one tap.

How does the price compare?

Routio is a flat $59.99/year (about $5/month) with a 7-day free trial — no per-month creep. Everlance offers a limited free tier and a paid plan around $8.99/month.

Do I need an account?

No sign-up, no password. Your data lives on your device and your private iCloud and syncs across your devices automatically.

Can I bring my Everlance history?

Yes. Export your Everlance trips as a CSV, then use Import history in Routio to bring your past drives in with their dates, miles, and business/personal split. You keep your whole year instead of starting fresh.
