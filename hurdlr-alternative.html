Questions

Hurdlr vs Routio.

I only want mileage — is Hurdlr overkill?

If your main need is tracking miles and getting a tax deduction, a full income/expense/tax-estimate app is more than you need. Routio focuses on mileage and an IRS Schedule C package — simpler to use and cheaper.

Does Routio make me re-classify every trip?

No. Routio auto-classifies based on your routes and learns over time, so most drives are sorted for you. You just confirm — instead of re-labeling everything that was marked "business" by default.

Do I have to link my bank?

Never. Routio is mileage-first and works with no bank connection and no account.

What does it cost?

A flat $59.99/year (about $5/month) with a 7-day free trial. No tiered finance features to pay up for.

Can I bring my Hurdlr history?

Yes. Export your Hurdlr trips as a CSV, then use Import history in Routio to bring your past drives in with their dates, miles, and business/personal split. You keep your whole year instead of starting over.
